For all affiliates with pending commissions to be received, please read the instructions we have for you below as they are extremely important in order for you to receive your payments.


As required by the US government, where we are legally registered, all affiliates must fill in IRS documents and send us the same properly filled in.


There are three (3) different documents (W-8Ben, W-8BEN-E & W9) you may be required to fill in and send to us in order to receive your commission, but you must fill in only one and for that, please see below what fits your profile (US-resident, Non-US resident, business, individual, etc). However these documents must be filled in only once (Normally the first time you will request a withdrawal).
